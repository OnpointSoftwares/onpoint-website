Our Story

Building the Future Together

Founded by Vincent Bett in 2020, OnPointsoftware solutions started with a heartfelt mission: helping students complete their final year projects and turn their academic dreams into reality.

What began as a passion for empowering students evolved into something bigger. By 2022, we partnered with Stairway to expand our vision—building professional websites for businesses and helping them establish their digital presence. Today, we're a proud, officially registered company (2026) serving clients worldwide with cutting-edge software solutions.
